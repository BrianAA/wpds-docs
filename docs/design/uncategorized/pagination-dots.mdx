# Pagination Dots

<!-- Description of component -->
<p className="font-xs font-light font--subhead">
Pagination reflects the user's progress through a set of steps, e.g. 
pages in a carousel or steps in a multi-step form. The "active" dot 
is darker and larger than the others, with steps further away decreasing in size.
</p>

## Behavior
<!-- Start of a guide -->

<Grid>
<Container>
<DefaultExample/>
</Container>
<div>

#### Dot transition 
When the current index changes the active dot will transition to the new index. 
The closer dots will transition to small dots while futher dots will transition to the smallest dots. 
The container will transition to the direction of the active dot.
</div>
</Grid>

<Grid>
<Container>
<LessThan5Example/>
</Container>
<div>

#### When total is < 5 
When there is less than 5 but more than 3 one dot will be the smallest
</div>
</Grid>

<Grid>
<Container>
<LessThan4Example/>
</Container>
<div>

#### When total is < 4 
When there is less than 4 all inactive dots will be the same size.
</div>
</Grid>

<Guide guidance="do" className="h-auto">

#### Use to show hidden items
Pagination dots are great indicators when items are part of a larger group that is hidden.
<br/>

    <div className="pa-lg bg-offblack brad-8">

#### Do not use on dark backgrounds 
Currently pagination dots have one color theme and cannot be used on dark backgrounds.
